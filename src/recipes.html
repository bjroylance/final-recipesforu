Ingredients

  • 30 oz. Frozen Hash Browns
  • 2 cups Sour Cream
  • 10.5 oz. Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 10 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Minced Onion
  • 2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Cups Corn Flakes

Instructions

  1. Allow potatoes to thaw in your fridge overnight, or spread them on a baking sheet and warm them in the oven at 200 degrees for about 20 minutes , until thawed.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Combine sour cream, cream of chicken soup, 6 Tablespoons of melted butter, salt, pepper and dried onion in a bowl. Mix well.
  4. Add potatoes and shredded cheese and stir to combine. Spoon mixture into a single layer in a 9x13'' pan.
  5. Add cornflakes to a large ziplock bag and crush gently with your hands or a rolling pin.
  6. Add remaining 4 tablespoons of melted butter to the crushed cornflakes and combine well. Sprinkle mixture over potatoes.
  7. Bake uncovered at 350 F for 40-50 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast Sliced 1-inch Cubes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 2 Cups Broccoli Florets
  • 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper Sliced 1-inch Pieces
  • 1/2 Red Bell Pepper Sliced 1-inch Pieces
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced Baby Carrots
  • 2 Teaspoons Minced Ginger
  • 2 Garlic Cloves Minced

    • Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Corn Starch
  • 2 Tablespoons Cold Water
  • 1/4 Cup Low Sodium Chicken Broth
  • 3 Tablespoons Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Honey
  • 1 Tablespoon Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper Flakes

Instructions

  1. In a medium size bowl, whisk together corn starch and water. Add remaining ingredients (chicken broth, soy sauce, honey, and toasted sesame oil, red pepper flakes) and whisk to combine. Set aside.
  2. Add one tablespoon of olive oil to a large skillet or wok and heat over medium high heat.
  3. Add chicken (in batches if necessary) and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from skillet.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add remaining tablespoon of oil to the skillet.
  5. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and carrots and cook, stirring occasionally, just until crisp tender. Add ginger and garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Add chicken back into the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Whisk stir fry sauce and pour over chicken and vegetables and stir gently to combine.
  8. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, and let boil for one minute.
  9. Serve with rice and/or chow mein if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 Loaf Texas Toast Bread
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced Provologne Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced Pepper Jack Cheese

Instructions

  1. Spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place a skillet over low to medium/low heat. A griddle should be at 275˚F. Immediately add 2 slices of bread with the butter-side-down.
  3. Stack cheeses on one of the pieces of bread, cover with the other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over.
  4. Continue sauteeing, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half on the diagonal to serve

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Cloved Garlic
  • 1 Sliced Green Pepper
  • 1 Diced Tomato
  • 1 Can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Can Tomato Paste
  • 2 Teaspoons Oregano
  • 2 Teaspoons Basil
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Avacodos
  • 1 Cup Chopped Tomato
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1/4 Cup Sliced Cilantro
  • 2 Teaspoons Lemon Juice
  • 1 Jalepeno

Instructions

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until creamy.
  2. Mix tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lemon juice, and jalapeño pepper into mashed avocado until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve with your choice of chips and salsa and enjoy!
